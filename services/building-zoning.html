Building & Zoning

Information about building permits, zoning regulations, land division, and construction requirements in Colfax Township.

Important: Colfax Township does NOT have its own zoning ordinance; however, building permits are still required and must be obtained from the Oceana County Building Department in Hart.

There IS zoning along the Pere Marquette River that is handled by the Michigan Department of Natural Resources.

Questions about permit requirements? Contact Supervisor David Leonard for guidance.
