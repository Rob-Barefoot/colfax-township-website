Building Department

Permits, inspections, and building regulations for Colfax Township

Building Services

The Building Department ensures safe construction practices and compliance with local building codes throughout Colfax Township.

Building Permits

Required for new construction, additions, renovations, and certain repairs. Permits ensure work meets safety standards and local codes.

  • New residential construction
  • Commercial building projects
  • Home additions and renovations
  • Accessory structures (sheds, garages)

Inspections

Required inspections at various stages of construction to ensure compliance with building codes and safety standards.

  • Foundation and footing inspections
  • Framing inspections
  • Electrical, plumbing, and mechanical
  • Final occupancy inspections

Code Enforcement

Enforcement of building codes, zoning regulations, and property maintenance standards to protect public safety.

  • Zoning compliance
  • Property maintenance standards
  • Violation investigations
  • Code compliance assistance

Plan Review

Review of construction plans and specifications to ensure compliance with applicable codes and regulations.

  • Architectural plan review
  • Site plan evaluation
  • Code compliance verification
  • Permit application processing

Permit Process

1

Application Submission

Submit complete application with required plans and documentation.

2

Plan Review

Plans are reviewed for code compliance and safety requirements.

3

Permit Issuance

Once approved, permit is issued and construction may begin.

4

Inspections

Required inspections are conducted during construction phases.

Inspection Requests

To schedule an inspection:

  • Call the building department
  • Provide permit number and inspection type
  • Schedule at least 24 hours in advance
