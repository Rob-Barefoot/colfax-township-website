Michigan Open Meetings Act

Colfax Township complies with the Michigan Open Meetings Act. All meetings are open to the public unless specifically exempt under the law. Meeting notices are posted in accordance with legal requirements.

If you need special accommodations to attend a meeting, please contact the township clerk at least 24 hours in advance.
