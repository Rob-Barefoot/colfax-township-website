The Colfax Township Board meets on the first Tuesday of each month at 7:00 pm.

Occasionally these dates must be changed to accommodate an election or if they fall on a holiday.

All meetings will be held at the Colfax Township Hall

5594 N 192nd Ave, Walkerville MI 49459

All township meetings are open to the public. We encourage community participation and welcome your input.

Meeting Information

How to Participate

  • Attend in person - no registration required
  • Speak during public comment period
  • Submit written comments to be read aloud
  • Contact board members directly

Meeting Accessibility

  • Township Hall is ADA compliant
  • Large print agendas available upon request
  • Sign language interpreter available with advance notice
  • Contact us for special accommodations
