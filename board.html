Board of Supervisors

Meet the elected officials who serve Colfax Township and represent our community's interests.

The Township Board consists of 5 elected members. Those members include the Supervisor, Clerk, Treasurer, and two Trustees. These 5 community members work together to ensure the statutory duties of the Township are accomplished.

Please remember these are personal phone numbers. Please do not call in the early mornings or late at night. Please do be civil and respectful. Remember, we're all neighbors.

Dave Leonard

Serving since: January 2022

About

Dave Leonard has served as township supervisor since 2022. He has been a Colfax property owner since 1991 and full-time resident since 2011. A graduate of Grand Valley State University, Dave served as a police officer for 27 years. While an officer, Dave was president of the Grand Rapids Police Officers Association and sat on the board of the Michigan Association of Police Organizations. After retiring from law enforcement, he authored a book about his police career.

In addition to his duties for Colfax Township, Dave is the current Chairperson of the Michigan Township Association Oceana County Chapter. He is an avid hunter and sports fan, especially of his beloved Detroit Tigers!
