About Colfax Township

Discover our community, services, and commitment to residents

Welcome to Colfax Township

Colfax Township is a rural community located in Oceana County, Michigan. We are committed to providing quality services to our residents while preserving the natural beauty and small-town character that makes our community special.

Our township government works diligently to maintain infrastructure, provide essential services, and ensure the safety and well-being of all residents. We strive to be responsive to community needs while managing resources responsibly.

Whether you're a long-time resident or new to the area, we encourage you to get involved in your local government and participate in the democratic process that shapes our community's future.

Location & Geography

Colfax Township is located in Oceana County, Michigan, in the western part of the state. The township encompasses rural and agricultural areas, providing a peaceful setting for residents who value country living while maintaining access to nearby urban amenities.

Key Features:

  • Rural agricultural community
  • Natural areas and wildlife habitat
  • Close proximity to Lake Michigan
  • Access to recreational opportunities

Township History

Colfax Township has a rich history as part of Michigan's agricultural heritage. The township was established as settlers moved into the area seeking fertile farmland and opportunities for rural living.

Over the years, the township has maintained its agricultural character while adapting to the changing needs of residents. Today, we continue to balance growth with preservation of our rural landscape and community values.

The township government has evolved to meet modern administrative requirements while maintaining the accessibility and personal service that residents expect from local government.
